About Me

Hi, I am Ashraf El Ghinoussi, mainly a front end and WordPress web developer/designer. I started as a young ethical hacker back in 2013, it was fun but I decided it was a waste of time, so I entered web development industry. Since then, I have helped small businesses, agencies, and individuals around the world to have an online presence by developing and designing their online platforms.

For most clients, I mostly use WordPress as I believe it's most often the best fit for my clients. Also being open source, and having a huge community is a huge plus. Other than WordPress, I am a master at 70% of front end technology, specially ReactJs which I used in this portfolio alongside NextJs for serverside rendering.

Using programs like Photoshop, tweaking a theme, finding edge CSS solutions, add subtle javascript animations, or automating things on the terminal is like second nature for me.

In my web development journey, I learned from Google's approach to their products that what matters the most for a successful website is a user experience driven design. That's why you will find that all my websites have an unusual beautiful design but still make you feel like you are at home.

If I am not in from of my beloved computer coding or working on my business, I will probably be learning as my life motto is "you start dying when you stop learning and improving".
